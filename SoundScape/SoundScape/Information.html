To play, you need a compatible Samsung Smart TV or Speaker. These can be identified by the following model numbers:

For 2014 TVs, H5500 and above, (Except for H6003/H6103/H6153/H6201/H6203).

For 2015 TVs, J4500, J5500 and above, (Except for J6200). Models 110S9 are also compatible.

For Speakers, WAM6500/1 and WAM7500/1

Both TV/Speaker and mobile need to be on the same WiFi Network. You are currently on mittest.

It takes a couple of minutes for a TV to be ready for connections after power on.
